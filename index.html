My Interests

Now my whole life, i have had way too many interests to count from. Including reading to cooking to running. New interest's were a way for me to experience more and to find new inspirations.

Now i have had a few interests that have stuck with me to this day that i will always love for their own reasons. They make me who i am and they are where i found and still find to this day my inspiration for all my work.

Now cars have been and always will be my favorite interest since they are the reason that got me into design. Cars have been part of my life ever since i was a kid, from watching shows about them, to studying them, to even taking photos and drawing sketches of my dream cars. The reason why i love them so much is that there is no boundaries when designing or styling your car. You can make it as you unique as you want to so that it represents you and what you like.

Another important interest to me is nature. Either doing activities in nature or just apreciating it. Its been somewhere i can always count on to bring me back to earth, bring me peace. From just a walk in the woods to the smell of dewey grass. It's almost like i step out of my head and into this brand new world. Nature has always been a second home to me and a place where i can gather and organise ideas for projects or any type of work.
